Our Story

The journey of flavors and passion.

  • 2009

    A Culinary Dream

    Our founder, Chef Maria, embarked on a culinary journey, experimenting with local ingredients and traditional recipes in her small home kitchen.

  • June 2011

    Our First Restaurant Opens

    After years of perfecting recipes and gathering a loyal following at local food markets, we opened our first small restaurant in the heart of the city.

  • 2015

    Farm-to-Table Initiative

    We launched our farm-to-table initiative, partnering with local farmers to source the freshest ingredients and support our community while elevating our dishes.
